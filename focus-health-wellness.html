Focus Area

Health & Wellness

Accessible, preventive, holistic, and science-informed wellbeing for communities.

The challenge

Healthcare often begins after illness arrives. Preventive knowledge — nutrition, daily wellbeing habits, mindfulness, and time-tested traditional wisdom — is frequently the least accessible to the communities that need it most.

Our approach

We focus on prevention and access. Our work combines traditional wellness knowledge, including India’s food wisdom and Ayurvedic traditions, with responsible, science-informed practice. Wellbeing should be a consistent daily practice — affordable, understandable, and dignified.

We do not make medical, clinical, or treatment claims. Where scientific innovation is relevant, it is presented responsibly and only with appropriate supporting evidence.

Areas of action

  • Preventive health awareness programmes
  • Nutrition and food-wisdom education
  • Mindfulness and balanced-lifestyle practice
  • Documentation of traditional wellness knowledge
  • Accessible community-health initiatives
  • Responsible, evidence-informed wellness innovation

Example initiative formats

  • Community wellness-awareness sessions
  • Preventive-health knowledge campaigns
  • Nutrition programmes connected to community kitchens
  • Wellness-knowledge documentation projects

Partnership opportunities

Healthcare and wellness experts, nutritionists, knowledge institutions, and community organisations can each contribute a specialised capability. See how partnership works →
