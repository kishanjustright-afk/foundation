Right Wellness

Wellbeing as a daily practice.

Right Wellness is a focused initiative under rightFOUNDATION — bringing together preventive wellbeing, traditional knowledge, responsible science, mindful living, and carefully considered wellness solutions.

What it covers

Tradition and science, brought together responsibly.

Ancient wisdom — including Ayurveda and India’s food traditions — considered alongside modern, evidence-informed practice. Solutions are curated carefully, never claimed beyond what can be supported.

A note on responsibility

Right Wellness supports wellbeing. It does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment, and it does not make clinical or product-performance claims. All wellness content is reviewed before publication, and where regulated topics arise, the appropriate health-authority requirements are respected.
