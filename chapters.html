India · UAE · Global

One philosophy. Growing chapters.

The Foundation is structured to scale through regional chapters that share one purpose while responding to local needs. The directions below are proposed areas of engagement.

Proposed focus

India

Community-rooted programmes in skills, wellness, education, and sustainable development — rooted in India’s knowledge traditions and confident in their global relevance.

Areas of engagement

  • Rural skill and content enablement for women and youth
  • Documentation of food wisdom and wellness traditions
  • Craft, artisan, and MSME enablement with economic dignity
  • Community wellness and preventive-health awareness

Areas of engagement

  • Cross-sector partnerships and responsible innovation
  • Multicultural community engagement
  • Regional collaboration on wellness and sustainability
  • Knowledge exchange between India and the Gulf region
Proposed focus

UAE

A hub for partnerships, innovation, and multicultural community engagement — connecting purpose-led work across the region.

Proposed focus

Global

Impact models that work locally deserve to be shared internationally — through knowledge exchange, responsible partnerships, and collaboration.

Areas of engagement

  • Knowledge exchange and open impact models
  • Responsible international partnerships
  • Scalable frameworks for self-sustaining ecosystems
  • Global collaboration on wellbeing and sustainability
